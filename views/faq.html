Frequently Asked Questions

Is ethic an insurance company ?

No, Ethic is not. You still have a carrier which will step in forlarger amounts.

Is ethic transparent ?

No, Ethic is not. You still have a carrier which will step in forlarger amounts.

What is a deductible and what does it have to do with Ethic ?

For example with car insurance, collision deductible means you'll pay out of pocket of the damage. The most common deductible is $500. By raising your deductible up to $2500 depending on your carrier, you pay considerably lower monthly premiums. And the additional out-of-pocket is covered by Ethic members. All you pay monthly is your fair share of other's claims.

Incase of a claim, do i have to put up with part of the amount ?

Yes. By means of avoiding fraud and abusive claims (which would harm our members) you still have an out-of-pocket on Ethic. For auto, this out-of-pocket is $250 or $500 depending on who your carrier is.

What does Ethic cover ?

For auto insurance, Ethic covers any damage resulting from an at-fault collision. In some cases, Ethic will cover you for non at-fault collisions. Rarer cases such as bodily injuries, theft and vandalism will still be covered by your insurance company.

What happens if the total damage is superior to your new deductible ?

Ethic will rapidly cover its share of the damage, and you will file an insurance claim with your traditional carrier so he covers the rest.
