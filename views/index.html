How it works

Ethic is a platform for consumers with similar property (auto, home...) to directly cover each other’s small claims, and thus rely less on insurance companies for coverage. This results in both smaller monthly premiums and less claims filed with your insurer !

What make Ethic so great

Lower premiums

By having a higher carrier deductible, you pay considerably less in premiums each month to your insurer. The only thing you pay on Ethic is a contribution to other’s claims. Nothing else.

Unchanged coverage

In the unwanted case you would have a claim, Ethic members will cover the first part of it, called the deductible. If it is a large claim, your carrier will kick in and cover the rest.

Better claim experience

Small claims should not require for you to go through you insurer’s cumbersome process. Claims on Ethic are not reviewed by our employees. They are reviewed by people like you and us who have no incentive against you.

Transparency

Ethic is built in such a way that the service is fully transparent and fair. We use blockchain technology to keep track of who paid what. In our view, part of the problem today with insurance companies is the lack of transparency: You have no idea where what you paid last month actually went. With Ethic and its technology, what is paid by members goes directly to covering members. And blockchain is the unquestionable proof of that.
