A new approach to mutual coverage

Ethic is a California-based company which started with the assessment that auto insurance is too expensive and opaque. Traditional insurers charge very high premiums, but policyholders don’t benefit as much as they should.

In 2014, for every dollar paid in premiums to the largest insurer in the US, only 63 cents actually went to covering damages to policyholders! Ethic brings a peer-to-peer architecture to the existing insurance landscape: consumers with similar profiles directly cover each other’s small claims, and thus rely on their insurer only for large amounts.

Think of it as a new kind of mutualization. This results in both smaller monthly premiums and less claims filed with your insurer!

When you join Ethic, we automatically match you to a pool of safe drivers with similar profiles to yours. If you wish to create your own pool with people you trust and have affinities with (people you went to school with, people who belong to your sports club, residents of your neighborhood...) we will help you *get set up* in no time.

If you get into say a collision and need coverage, members inside your pool will directly cover it in equal proportions, up to a certain amount.

If the amount of your claim is substantial, your own insurer will step in to cover the rest. You reciprocally agree to cover your share of claims filed by other drivers inside your pool. The less the group claims, the more members save. Ethic makes coverage more humane and less expensive, without having to spend hours shopping around to change carriers!

What's in it for you?

Lower premiums

By having a higher deductible, you pay considerably less in premiums each month to your insurer. The only thing you pay on Ethic is a contribution to others' claims. Nothing else.

Unchanged coverage

In the unwanted case you would have a claim, Ethic members will cover it up to a certain amount. If it is a large claim, your insurer will kick in and cover the rest.

Better claim experience

Small claims require flexibility. You shouldn't have to go through a cumbersome process. Claims are reviewed by our qualified, independant and unbiased partners.

Our technology serves transparency

Ethic is built in such a way that the service is fully transparent and fair. We use block chain technology to keep track of claims and monthly payments. In our view, part of the problem today with insurance companies is the lack of transparency: you have no idea where your money actually goes. With Ethic and its technology, what is paid by members goes directly to covering claims. And our use of the block chain is the unquestionable proof of that.
